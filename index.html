I'm born and bred in Elephant and Castle, South East London. I am of Ghanaian descent. I'm a keen sportsman who has grown up playing sports for the majority of my life. This has moulded me to be a competitive person who loves challenges.

I believe a healthy body equals a healthy mind; therefore, I try to workout 3 times per week. I studied Sport and Exercise Science at London South Bank University whilst playing Semi-professional football. I'm a massive fan of crime dramas/thrillers and my all-time favourite series is The Wire; where Idris Elba had his big break. I love to travel and explore different cultures; my goal is to visit 1 new country per year.

I also aim to learn French and Spanish in the next 5 years as I believe you learn more about people when conversating in their native tongue. I'm currently working as a Business Development Consultant for a Food Tech company called Nutritics.

Why Web Development?

Talk about why you'd like to move into a career as a web developer

My competitive nature and a love for challenges and solving puzzles will help me thrive as a web developer. No day is ever the same as there are always problems to fix and you get a great sense of accomplishment when a task is completed.

I love to travel, so the flexibility in being able to work anywhere is what I'm attracted to. The clear career path progression and high earning potential is another reason why I want to be become a successful Web developer. I feel like I haven't lived life, I have just been surviving, and now I want to make a difference and build something that leaves a legacy. The industry is currently booming so there are various opportunities waiting for me.

Many of my friends would say that I am a tech geek because I love innovation and I'm always intrigued with new technology.
